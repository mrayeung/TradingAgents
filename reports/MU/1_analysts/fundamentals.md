# Fundamental Analysis Report: Micron Technology, Inc. (MU)
**Date:** 2026-05-04

---

## 1. Executive Summary
Micron Technology, Inc. (`MU`) is a leading player in the semiconductor industry, specializing in memory and storage solutions. As of May 4, 2026, the company shows strong financial recovery following a challenging 2023. With substantial growth in revenue and net income in the most recent fiscal year (ended Aug 31, 2025), Micron demonstrates resilience in a cyclical industry. However, traders should be cautious of the inherent volatility in the semiconductor sector and the company's high debt-to-equity ratio.

## 2. Company Profile
Micron Technology is a global leader in memory and storage solutions. Through its global brands—Micron and Crucial—it delivers high-performance DRAM, NAND, and NOR Flash memory products essential for data centers, cloud infrastructure, mobile devices, automotive, and industrial applications.

## 3. Financial Analysis

### Income Statement Highlights
*   **Revenue Recovery:** After a trough in fiscal 2023 ($15.54 billion), revenue has rebounded strongly to $37.38 billion in fiscal 2025.
*   **Profitability:** The company shifted from a significant net loss in 2023 (-$5.83 billion) to a robust net income of $8.54 billion in 2025, reflecting improved market conditions and operational efficiency.
*   **Margins:** Operating margin stands at approximately 67.6%, indicating strong control over costs relative to revenue growth.

### Balance Sheet Overview
*   **Financial Health:** Micron maintains a healthy liquidity position with a current ratio of 2.90, suggesting it can comfortably cover its short-term liabilities.
*   **Capital Structure:** The company holds significant debt ($15.28 billion total debt) resulting in a high debt-to-equity ratio of 14.9, which is a key area of risk for conservative investors.
*   **Asset Growth:** Total assets have grown steadily to $82.80 billion, primarily driven by substantial investments in property, plant, and equipment (Net PPE: $47.33 billion).

### Cash Flow Analysis
*   **Operating Cash Flow:** Operating cash flow reached a strong $17.53 billion in 2025, up from $8.51 billion in 2024 and $1.56 billion in 2023.
*   **CapEx:** Capital expenditures remain high ($15.86 billion in 2025) as the company continues to invest in advanced manufacturing capabilities and capacity expansion.
*   **Free Cash Flow (FCF):** Despite high CapEx, the company generated $1.67 billion in FCF in 2025, highlighting improved cash generation capabilities.

## 4. Key Valuation Ratios

*   **Forward PE:** 5.68 (suggesting expected significant earnings growth)

## 5. Actionable Insights for Traders
1.  **Strong Growth Potential:** The extremely low PEG ratio (0.19) and attractive Forward PE (5.68) suggest that if Micron maintains its growth trajectory, the stock may currently be undervalued by the market.
2.  **Monitor Cyclicality:** The semiconductor memory industry is notoriously cyclical. Traders should monitor demand trends in data centers and AI, as these are primary drivers for MU's top-line growth.
3.  **Debt Risk:** The high debt-to-equity ratio warrants close attention. Any sudden downturn in the memory market could make servicing this debt more challenging.
4.  **Technical Momentum:** The stock is trading above its 200-day moving average ($279.33), indicating a long-term bullish trend.
